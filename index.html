About Us

Slacklining refers to the act of balanced walking on a flat webbing that is tensioned between two anchors.

The Delhi based SLacktivism slackline community, started in 2013 by Enrico Fabian. The community comes together almost every Sunday. Started in parks, based on weather the community holds regular sessions for Waterline and Highline in outskirts of Delhi.

Interested to to Slack your way on line, look-up for weekly updates on Instagram or Facebook
